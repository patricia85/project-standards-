How to use this Excel Risk Dashboard:
  1. Enter your risk data in the "Risk Register" table below
  2. For Impact and Likelihood, use values from 1-5 (1=lowest, 5=highest)
  3. Risk levels will automatically update based on the Risk Matrix
  4. Update the summary cards manually based on your risk counts
  5. Save this file as an Excel workbook (.xlsx) for future updates
